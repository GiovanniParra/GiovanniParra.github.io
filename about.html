The Story

I create things. Sometimes that means writing Python scripts to audit tax residency, and other times it means designing redstone contraptions in Minecraft. My philosophy is simple: build tools that I actually need, make them look good, and make them work for everyday people.

My path wasn't linear. I was born in El Paso, TX, but spent 14 years growing up in Mexico before moving to Fresno, CA to live with my uncle. Now at Berkeley, I’m using code to figure out my purpose in the world. Every project I ship is just me trying to solve a problem while figuring out who I want to be.

When I'm not in VS Code, you can usually find me at the gym working out, woodworking, or exploring nature. I love the process of creating offline—whether that's practicing my guitar skills, editing video in Premiere Pro, or just pencil sketching. This year, my main project is just "building me."
